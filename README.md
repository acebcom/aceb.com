## What is ACEB?

ACEB is a next-generation digital marketplace that bridges the gap between cryptocurrency and everyday spending. Instead of navigating complex exchanges or paying high conversion fees, ACEB lets you spend your crypto directly on gift cards from hundreds of leading global brands.

Whether you're shopping online, streaming your favorite shows, ordering food, booking travel, or sending a digital gift to someone across the world — ACEB gives you an instant, borderless, and reliable way to use your digital currency.
